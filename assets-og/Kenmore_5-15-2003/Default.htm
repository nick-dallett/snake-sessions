AlexL visits Seattle

5-15-2003, AlexL met me at The Bloc in Kenmore. The Bloc is closing down after 3 months due to a nasty landlord and lack of income. The owner invited the crew from OMLB to come down and skate for free the last weekend the park was open. I invited Alex to come join us. Unfortunately, only Steve and I showed from OMLB.
