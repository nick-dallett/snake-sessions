Ballard Bowl, 12-26 and 12-27, 2003

A couple of mellow sessions, with Max on the 26th, and with Shawn, Hughes, Greg, and Andy on the 27th. On the 27th, Shawn earned yet another moniker - "Tough in Teal" - as he showed up in a teal track suit in preparation for a footrace with an Ethiopian runner that he challenged in a local bar. The challenger never showed up, so Shawn "The Bullet" "Tough in Teal" "Rain Man" McLean skated instead.
