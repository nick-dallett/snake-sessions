Milton, 5-8-2004

Opening day at the new Milton skatepark drew a bunch of us oldsters for the early session. I was there around 6:30 AM, and Dan got there about 30 minutes later. Pete, Dave, and Doug showed up next, and then the local kids started arriving. Then Ryan showed up and started putting us all to shame. Ryan has skated Milton 3 times before (and got busted each time :-))
