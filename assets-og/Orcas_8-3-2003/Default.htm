Weekend in the San Juans - August 2nd and 3rd, 2003

I threw my bike and a backpack on the Anacortes ferry and rode out to Eastsound on Orcas Island to skate the Grindline park there. snapped some shots of Some of the skaters there. In typical Nick fashion, I didn't take names (fitting, as I didn't kick ass, either), so excuse misnames or spelling errors :-).
