Assembling a skateboard wheel

Here's the steps to putting a skateboard wheel together. The spacer and the speed washers are important - they work together to create a solid metal connection through the wheel. This prevents deformation of the wheel and increases speed, stability, and the life of the wheels and bearings. With spacers and speed washers in place, you can tighten the axle nut all the way down and still get full speed from your wheels.

The bearing assembly, as it looks without the wheel

The pieces you'll need


The naked wheel
1. Pressing in the first bearing with a bearing tool (a screwdriver will work in its place)
2. Add the spacer
3. Push the second bearing in the other side, locking the spacer inside

4. Put the inner speed washer on the axle
5. Slip the wheel on, and 6. put the outer speed washer on top
7. Finally, thread on the axle nut and tighten it down.
