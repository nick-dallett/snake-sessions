Liberty Park, Renton - Sept 6, 2003

Matt and I went for a morning session at Renton. I worked on my 5-0 to tail and handplants on the small wall, and took my backside rock and roll to the big wall of the "toilet bowl."

I was quite sore after practicing the handplants for a while... new muscles coming into play.

Watching myself do rock and rolls is frustrating, since I'm just tapping the board on the coping, not getting the extension I'd like. More to work on :-)
