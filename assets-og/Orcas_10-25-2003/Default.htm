Bring Yer Own Session - Orcas 10-25-03

Early in the morning on 10/25, I met up with Dan "Darth" Hughes and his kid Jeremy for a skate trip to Orcas [aka Orcatraz, The Rock, Raucous Island]. We picked up a few fellow expeditioners on the way - Max, Carl ("AuggieDawg"), Matt ("Chowdah"), and Fireman John. We also met up a crew from Portland - Steve Gump, Tom Miller, and two of their friends. On the ferry, we hooked up with Doug, a couple or three Mikes, Rob and Mrs. Skala, Joe, and Bill. All told about a venti load of Sleestaks to session the rock.

I got some good pix, and a few videos. Sadly, I didn't get nearly all the vids I wanted. All I got of Joe and Auggie were bail runs, so I stuck them at the end.

I couldn't quite figure out how to photograph Auggie's skating - what kind of shot will capture a hardflip to tail, to revert?

My camera is pretty tough to figure out. Dan got a few shots of me, but not precisely the shots he wanted :-).

All in all, a great session with some extremely sick skating.
