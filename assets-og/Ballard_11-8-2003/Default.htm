Ballard Bowl, 11-8-2003

A warm sunny morning after a week of cold ones. George and I squeegeed the bowl dry, and then they came out of the woodwork to sk8. After a couple of nasty slams, I grabbed the camera and shot some of the action. Surprise of the day - Shawn "camera shy" McLean agreeing to have his picture taken :-O

I had my best session yet. Now if only someone would figure out how to use my camera :-).
