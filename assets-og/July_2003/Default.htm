Ballard Bowl 6-28-03

I got Shawn to snap a few shots of me, and got some nice shots myself of some other guys who were ripping the place apart. The high point of the day was hitting coping in the deep end for the first time ever - a frontside wheeler on the North wall. so far, I've only done it once, and it was a complete surprise. Just so happens there was a guy there with a couple of cameras - looked professional - who got a pic of it. I was stoked. gave him my email address, but he has yet to mail me the pic :-(.

Fourth of July, Matt's ramp

Matt from OMLB invited the gang to his place for a big BBQ for the 4th of July. There were lots of babes (including my wife and daughter :-)), a miniramp, and a plethora of viands. Jim and I skated briefly, Matt had a short sesh, and Jim and I gave Payton a few pointers. It's the first time Payton let me put her on the board and run her back and forth up the walls of the ramp :-)

After this, Matt's band set up on the ramp and played a few tunes. Not sure I've ever seen a band play on a mini before :-)

July 12 at Renton

Jeff from OMLB was at Renton Saturday morning as usual, and after we'd skated for a while and I was warmed up, he trotted out his digi and snapped xome pix of me doing some FS grinds and airs in the toilet bowl and then a few pix in the tight little "impossi-bowl" pool in back. I was having more fun in the pool than ever before - really starting to get comfortable - and I started hitting frontside grinds there for the first time - my first FS grinds on real vert! Jeff got pix of me grinding in the pool on my Comet (Comet Proflex Park 36")
